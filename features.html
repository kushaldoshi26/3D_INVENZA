Core Platform

Platform Features

Every tool you need, refined to perfection.

◈

Holographic Preview

Real-time 3D viewer powered by Three.js. Rotate, zoom, and inspect your model from any angle before printing. Supports STL, OBJ, and 3MF.

☰

Layer-by-Layer Slicer

Slide through every layer of your print. Visualise walls, infill, supports, and rafts. Catch issues before they cost you material.

⚡

Neural Engine

AI-powered G-code optimisation that reduces fabrication time by up to 35% while maintaining tolerances of ±0.05mm.

◆

12+ Materials

PLA, PLA+, ABS, PETG, TPU, ASA, HIPS, Nylon, Standard Resin, Tough Resin, Carbon Fiber, and Wood PLA — with real-time cost calculation.

☁

Live Dashboard

Track your print live: G-code stream, layer progress bar, temperatures, and print time remaining — all in real time.

♾

Dental & Medical

Dedicated upload workflow for dental impression models. Supports DFM (Design for Manufacturing) analysis for precise medical parts.

◐

Instant Cost Estimate

Configure material, layer height, and infill and see the price update in real-time — no surprises at checkout.

✉

Order Tracking

Every order gets a timeline: Queued → Printing → Quality Check → Dispatched → Delivered. Stay updated at every step.

🎓

Student Program

Free personalised keychain for every registered student. 15% discount on all prints with a valid student ID.
